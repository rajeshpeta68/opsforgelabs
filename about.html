About OpsForge Labs

Bridge theory and enterprise engineering

OpsForge Labs was created to bridge the gap between academic learning and real enterprise engineering. Most graduates struggle because traditional learning focuses heavily on theory while modern companies demand practical problem-solving skills.

Our mission

We focus on preparing engineers for real environments — not just certifications.

Hands-on DevOps training

Build muscle memory with labs that reflect how teams ship and operate.

Real-world infrastructure exposure

Networks, clusters, pipelines, and observability — experienced in context.

AI-powered operations knowledge

Automation, triage, and modern tooling without hype.

Production troubleshooting experience

Incidents, rollbacks, and debugging under realistic constraints.

Vision

Industry-ready engineers

To build industry-ready DevOps and AI engineers capable of operating modern production systems confidently.

Mission pillars

  • Deliver practical engineering education
  • Simulate real enterprise scenarios
  • Teach AI-assisted operations
  • Help students become production-ready
